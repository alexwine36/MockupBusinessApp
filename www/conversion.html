Conversion Helper

The goal of this feature is simple. Help make mistakes with conversions a thing of the past.

 

How-To

The functionality of this Farad converter is relatively straight forward.

  1. Select the input field you would like to convert from
  2. Then change the focus by selecting an area outside of the input field or by pressing tab
  3. Watch as the fields are populated with all of the conversions
